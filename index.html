Welcome to non-plan

Innovating AEC with Digital Solutions

Based in Zurich, non-plan is a leading provider of cutting-edge software solutions tailored to the architecture, engineering, and construction (AEC) industry. Our mission is to enhance the efficiency, accuracy, and creativity of professionals by providing custom-made digital tools that fit seamlessly into their workflows. Our services include custom web applications, Python automation scripts, AR-based visualization tools, and much more.

Introducing the non-plan Platform

The Non-Plan WebApp is designed to simplify and accelerate project management in the AEC industry. It integrates a range of advanced tools to streamline workflows and improve productivity, from project tracking and task management to automated checks and feasibility assessments. Our web app empowers teams to track progress, collaborate seamlessly, and make data-driven decisions faster than ever before. The app also includes sophisticated capabilities such as automated plausibility modeling based on location and the generation of new geometries to visualize sustainable building practices.

Tailored Solutions for AEC Professionals

At Non-Plan, we don't just build tools – we create solutions that address the unique challenges of the AEC industry. Whether you're working in architecture, engineering, or construction, our services can help optimize your processes. We offer:

  • Custom web applications that integrate into your existing workflows.
  • Grasshopper and Python automation scripts to streamline repetitive tasks.
  • Advanced BIM and CAD consulting for creating and managing intelligent building models.
  • Specialized AR tools for visualizing designs and data in immersive environments.
